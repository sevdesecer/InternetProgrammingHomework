--- About Us ---

Eskişehir Technical University, established by Law No. 7141 published in the Official Gazette dated 18 May 2018, is located in the center of Eskişehir, a city known for its science, culture, and vibrant youth. The university encompasses 5 faculties, 2 vocational schools, 1 college, and 3 institutes, along with 6 research and application centers across 3 campuses. With a strong infrastructure for education, research, and development, Eskişehir Technical University is committed to preparing its students as the most competent and competitive individuals for the future, fostering creativity and dynamism in their educational environments. Notably, the university's Aviation and Space Sciences Faculty manages the operations of the international Hasan Polatkan Airport located on the 2nd September Campus, where various international flights by different airlines operate on different days of the week.
